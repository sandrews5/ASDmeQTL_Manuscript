There are 2 main parts to the GWAS loci expansion analysis. 

1) Annotate ASD-related SNPs in PGC with gene and meQTL information across
	tissue type. Also note the genes implicated by the meQTL targets of these SNPs. 
		Script: GLE_Annotation.r

2) Visually demonstrate the utility of GWAS loci expansion by plotting
	meQTLs in gene space across tissue type. 
		Script: GLE_Plotting.r
